Good Chicago Bars for Meetups

  • Sheffields

    Lakeview

    Sheffields is easy to get to, and has an outdoor patio for nights with nice weather. There's also a spacious backroom when it's cold. Great beer list, plus they serve food.

    Contributed by Savannah

  • Map Room

    Bucktown

    Map Room has good beer and free pretzels! And if you come there early in the day sometimes there is charcuterie lying around.

    Contributed by Cat

  • The Drifter

    River North

    Awesome cocktails and performances every hours!

    Contributed by Brian Montana

  • Simone's

    Andersonville

    Wonderful dive bar with live music on Sundays and good people watching.

    Contributed by Brian Montana
